About Neon Kenya

Fashion designer and model passionate about blending Kenyan culture with contemporary design.

Born and raised in Kenya, I draw inspiration from the vibrant colors of matatu culture, the serene beauty of our coastline, and the dynamic energy of urban Nairobi. My collections tell stories that celebrate African aesthetics while embracing global fashion trends.

From runway shows in Nairobi to editorial shoots across East Africa, I've had the privilege of working with various brands and photographers who share my vision of authentic, bold fashion.
